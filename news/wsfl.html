WSFL


Western Southern Financial Life Converts ClaimFacts


WSFL wanted to migrate from its legacy system, Erisco’s Claim Facts, to TriZetto’s Facets 4.51 application since Claim Facts was no longer going to be supported.  Unlike most major health plan organizations that use Facets, Western & Southern Financial Life (WSFL) was primarily a life insurance company that did not pursue external health plan clients or members.  They used this software to simply provide health plan coverage to all of their employees, spouses, and dependents.


abcs provided data conversion assistance to insure that the data transferred from the Claim Facts system would be properly inserted into the Facets Data Model.  Comparison scripts and reports were created to verify that the data had been completely and accurately transferred.
